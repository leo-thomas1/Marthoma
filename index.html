About Horeb Mar Thoma Church

In the early days, the church that Syrian Christians of Thiruvalla congregated for worship was at Niranam, which was one of the seven churches established by St. Thomas at Malankara: Kodungalloor, Kollam, Paloor, Paravoor, Thekkanpallippuram, Niranam, and Nilackal.

Later, a cathedral church was established at Thiruvalla Paliekkara in 1815 A.D. and holy services were conducted there. This church was burned and destroyed by the Pathtillathil Pottis, the lords of those days. Half of this amount was used to renovate the church, which is the present-day Paliekkara Church.
